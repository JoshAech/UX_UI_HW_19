    About Us

    We are a small gaming community that developed from our passion to want a place to be able to play and have fun with friends.

    We have carefully curated our location to be able to have several open tables available for those that are experienced and for those that are new to the world of table top gaming. Our main focus is Dungeon & Dragons, however we do offer several other games that are of the table top variety. We have Settlers of Catan, Pandemic, Descent: Legends of the Dark, Arkham Horror: The Card Game, Blood Rage, and Magic the Gathering among many more other games. We also have 3D printing services where we can print out your Minis so that you can play with them during your campaing. If you don't feel like playing but still want to been in this enviorment that we have created for you all, come and chat with us we love to talk to our patrons!
